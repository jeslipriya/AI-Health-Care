First Aid Guide

General First Aid Tips

Here are some essential first aid tips that everyone should know:

  • CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation): Learn how to perform CPR to save someone experiencing cardiac arrest.
  • Choking: Use the Heimlich maneuver to help someone who is choking.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure to stop bleeding and elevate the injured area if possible.
  • Burns: Cool the burn with running water for at least 10 minutes and cover it with a sterile dressing.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the injured area and avoid moving the person unless necessary.
  • Poisoning: Call emergency services immediately and provide information about the poison if known.
  • Heatstroke: Move the person to a cooler place, hydrate them, and apply cool compresses.
  • Hypothermia: Warm the person gradually using blankets and warm drinks.

First Aid Kit Essentials

Every home and workplace should have a well-stocked first aid kit. Here are the essentials:

  • Adhesive bandages (various sizes)
  • Sterile gauze pads and adhesive tape
  • Antiseptic wipes and ointment
  • Scissors and tweezers
  • Disposable gloves
  • Thermometer
  • Pain relievers (e.g., ibuprofen, acetaminophen)
  • Emergency blanket
  • First aid manual or instruction booklet

Emergency Contacts
